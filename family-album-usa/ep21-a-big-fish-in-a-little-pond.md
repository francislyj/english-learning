# Episode 21: A Big Fish in a Little Pond

## 场景概述

某位角色面临选择：在小地方做"大人物"，还是去大城市从头开始。涉及生活方式选择、城市 vs 小镇、权衡利弊。

## 核心句型

| 句型 | 用途 | 例句 |
|------|------|------|
| a big fish in a little pond | 小池塘里的大鱼（习语） | — 在小范围内很突出 |
| I'd rather + 动词 | 我宁愿… | I'd rather stay here. |
| What matters most is... | 最重要的是… | What matters most is happiness. |
| There's more opportunity in... | …有更多机会 | There's more opportunity in the city. |
| I'm torn between... | 我在…之间纠结 | I'm torn between staying and leaving. |
| Where do you see yourself in five years? | 五年后你想在哪里？ | — 思考未来方向 |

## 重点词汇

| 词/短语 | 释义 | 语境 |
|---------|------|------|
| big fish in a little pond | 在小圈子里很出众的人 | 本集标题习语 |
| small fish in a big pond | 在大环境里不起眼的人 | 对比表达 |
| settle down | 定居、安定下来 | settle down in a small town |
| pace of life | 生活节奏 | The pace of life is slower here. |
| trade-off | 取舍 | There's always a trade-off. |
| ambition | 抱负 | career ambition |

## 语法点

1. **I'd rather + 动词原形** — I'd rather live in a small town. / I'd rather not move.
2. **比较级对比** — The city is more exciting but more expensive.
3. **What matters is...** — What matters is what makes you happy. (主语从句)

## 文化注释

- **Big fish in a little pond** — 经典英语习语，暗示可能缺乏更大挑战
- **美国城乡差异** — 大城市 (NYC, LA) vs. 郊区/小镇是美国人常面临的选择
- **American Dream** — 去大城市追梦是美国文化经典叙事
