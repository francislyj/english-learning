# Episode 22: Career Choices

## 场景概述

面临职业转换或新方向的选择。涉及职业规划、追求梦想 vs 稳定收入、征求意见。

## 核心句型

| 句型 | 用途 | 例句 |
|------|------|------|
| I'm thinking about changing careers. | 我在考虑转行 | — 提出转变想法 |
| Follow your dream. | 追随你的梦想 | — 鼓励冒险 |
| What if it doesn't work out? | 万一不成功怎么办？ | — 表达担忧 |
| You'll never know unless you try. | 不试你永远不知道 | — 鼓励尝试 |
| It's never too late to... | 做…永远不晚 | It's never too late to start over. |
| I need to take a risk. | 我需要冒个险 | — 下定决心 |

## 重点词汇

| 词/短语 | 释义 | 语境 |
|---------|------|------|
| career change | 转行 | make a career change |
| passion | 热情、热爱 | Follow your passion. |
| stable / stability | 稳定的 / 稳定性 | a stable income |
| risk | 风险 | take a risk |
| pursue | 追求 | pursue your dream |
| regret | 后悔 | I don't want to have regrets. |
| fulfilling | 有成就感的 | a fulfilling career |

## 语法点

1. **What if + 从句?** — What if I fail? / What if it works? (假设性担忧)
2. **It's never too late to + 动词** — It's never too late to learn something new.
3. **think about + doing** — I'm thinking about going back to school.

## 文化注释

- **Career change** — 美国人一生平均换 5-7 次工作，转行不被视为失败
- **Follow your passion** — 美国文化鼓励追求热爱的事，即使有经济风险
- **Going back to school** — 美国成年人回学校读书很常见，没有年龄歧视
